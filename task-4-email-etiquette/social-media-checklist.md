# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before you post and ensure your content is respectful.
- Maintain a professional profile (clear bio, appropriate profile picture).
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content.
- Share informative, positive, and meaningful content.
- Be respectful and polite in comments and messages.

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
- Avoid spreading misinformation or unverified news.
- Don’t post content that can harm your future career or reputation.
